Travel Information

All iSLC Conference-related travel expenses will be covered for each Center’s selected participants. This includes airfare and ground transportation to and from the airport, hotel accommodations, and appropriate per diem allowances for travel with expected arrival date on Thursday, March 6, 2014 and departure date on Sunday, March 8, 2014. Based on our NSF-approved budget, each center has been allocated funding that should cover the costs of 15 participants to attend the conference.

Conference Venue

The 2012 iSLC Conference will be held at Carnegie Mellon University Center.

Airfare

Please book your flight using the cheapest avenue. Keep your receipts and submit them for reimbursement. All receipts need to be submitted by March 22nd or you will not be reimbursed.

Hotel Accommodations

All conference participants will stay at the Wyndham Pittsburgh University Center. To stay within our budget, we ask that all participants share a room. Participants who do not have a roommate may be asked to room with a participant from another Center who is of the same gender. Roommate preferences should be submitted to your Center’s iSLC Conference representative. You do not need to make a hotel reservation; this will be taken care of by the Local Planning Committee in Pittsburgh.
